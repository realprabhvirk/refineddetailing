Refined Detailing is a fully mobile service operating across Holmview and surrounding areas. We pull up to your driveway, your workplace, wherever — and we get to work. No waiting rooms, no booking a lift home, no wasted half-days. Just your car, looked after properly.

We use professional-grade products and equipment on every job. Whether it's a daily driver that needs a reset or a weekend car you want kept immaculate — we treat every vehicle like it matters.
